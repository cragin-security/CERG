## 2. Standard Qualification Responses

### Q: What framework do you use?

*We use **CERG**, an open-source cybersecurity operating model built on NIST CSF and NIST 800-53. It provides 100 Core Controls with tiered implementation guidance written for IT generalists, MSPs, and security engineers. Unlike proprietary frameworks, CERG is transparent — you can inspect every control, every recommendation, and every compensating control path.*

### Q: How do you scope engagements?

*We use a 4-tier engagement model based on the client's regulatory pressure and maturity:*

| Tier | Best For | Duration | Investment |
|------|----------|----------|------------|
| T1: Foundations | No regulatory pressure, basic hygiene | 2 weeks | $10-20k |
| T2: Structure | Basic compliance needs | 4 weeks | $30-50k |
| T3: Compliance | Active regulatory requirements | 6-8 weeks | $60-120k |
| T4: Strategic Partner | Multi-regulator, continuous improvement | 12-16 weeks | $150-300k |

### Q: What's your methodology?

1. **Intake** — Client context, regulatory pressure, sector overlay (1 day)
2. **Discovery** — Control assessment against 100 Core Controls, evidence collection (2-5 days)
3. **Tier Selection** — Scope finalization based on findings (1 day)
4. **Delivery** — Implementation per CERG controls with tool deployment, policy creation, process definition (2-16 weeks)
5. **Handover** — Documentation, evidence package, runbook, QBR schedule (1-2 days)

### Q: What evidence do you produce?

*Every control implemented produces current-state evidence: policy documents, configuration exports, audit logs, scan results. Evidence is timestamped and organized per control ID.*

### Q: Do you work with existing tools?

*Yes. We maintain an opinionated tool matrix with Primary / Acceptable / Avoid recommendations per control, but we **always** prefer your existing tool stack when it meets the control requirement.*

### Q: What post-engagement support do you provide?

- Monthly status reports + evidence packages
- Quarterly business reviews (QBRs) with maturity scorecard
- Annual tabletop facilitation
- Regulatory change monitoring + advisory
