# Pricing Anchor Model v1
## Practice Asset — Not a CERP Corpus Document

---

## Pricing Principles

1. **Fixed-price for defined scope.** Each tier has a defined deliverable set. The client pays a fixed price for that set. This removes the "how many hours will this take?" uncertainty.
2. **Advisory day rate for undefined work.** Anything outside the defined deliverable set is charged at a standard day rate. The day rate is disclosed at engagement signing.
3. **Phased billing for longer engagements.** Tier 3 and 4 engagements use phased billing tied to milestone completion, not calendar time.

## Pricing by Tier

### Tier 1: Foundations

| Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Fixed engagement fee | $X0,000 |
| Advisory day rate (post-handover) | $2,500/day |
| Duration | 30 days |
| Milestone billing | 50% at signing, 50% at handover |

**What's included:** Full MVC adoption, org profile build, policy sign-off, risk register bootstrap, first risk review, handover package.

**Typical effort:** 15-20 practitioner days over 30 calendar days.

### Tier 2: Structure

| Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Fixed engagement fee | $XX,000 |
| Advisory day rate (post-engagement) | $2,500/day |
| Duration | 90 days |
| Milestone billing | 50% at signing, 25% at day 45, 25% at handover |

**What's included:** Everything in Tier 1, plus standards gap analysis, architecture review activation, exposure management cycle, access management adoption, metrics dashboard draft, second maturity assessment.

**Typical effort:** 35-50 practitioner days over 90 calendar days.

### Tier 3: Compliance-to-Operations

| Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Engagement fee (base) | $XX,000 |
| Per-regulatory-package fee | $X,000 per package |
| Advisory day rate | $2,500/day |
| Ongoing advisory (quarterly retainer) | $X,000/quarter |
| Duration | 180 days implementation + optional ongoing advisory |
| Milestone billing | 30% at signing, 30% at day 90, 30% at handover, 10% at 30-day post-handover check |

**What's included:** Everything in Tier 2, plus regulatory package implementation, SCPs, evidence library, testing cadence, deficiency workflow, IR interface, third maturity assessment.

**Typical effort:** 60-100 practitioner days over 180 calendar days, plus regulatory package depth.

### Tier 4: Strategic Partner

| Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Annual retainer | $XXX,000/year |
| Quarterly billing | $XX,000/quarter |
| Strategic advisory day rate | $3,000/day (for unscheduled work) |
| Duration | 12-month renewable |

**What's included:** Everything in Tier 3, plus operating model evolution roadmap, multi-regulator governance design, workforce architecture, guild co-design, M&A integration, QBRs, board briefings.

**Typical effort:** 30-50 practitioner days per quarter (dedicated access).

## Expense Policy

- Travel: billed at cost with itemized receipts
- Software licenses required for engagement delivery: billed at cost with client pre-approval
- Subcontractor fees: included in engagement fee; no separate charge

---

## Price Adjustment Factors

| Factor | Adjustment | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Multiple concurrent regulatory packages | +15-25% per package | Tier 3 with PCI + SOX = +25% |
| Urgent start (< 2 weeks from signing) | +15% | Engagement starts within 14 days |
| Client provides no existing documentation | +10-15% | Starting from scratch means more adaptation work |
| Client has existing CERG knowledge | -10% | Client understands the model; less teaching time |
| Multi-year commitment | -10-15% per year | 2-year Tier 4 retainer |
