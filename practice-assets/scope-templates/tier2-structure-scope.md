## In Scope
- Everything in Tier 1 (In Scope), plus:
- Standards gap analysis and layered adoption recommendation
- Architecture review procedure (`CERG-PRC-AR-001`) activation with first 2 projects routed
- Exposure management cycle (`CERG-PRC-VM-001`) activation with first SLA report
- Access management standard (`CERG-STD-AC-001`) + onboarding runbook (`PRC-AC-002`) adoption
- CUI/CMMC, SOX, or PCI overlay selection and system-to-package mapping
- First metrics dashboard draft
- Second maturity assessment at day 90
- Two cadence cycles completed and documented

## Out of Scope
- Full regulatory operational package implementation (Tier 3)
- Tooling configuration or deployment
- Penetration testing
- Ongoing advisory — optional add-on

6. Regulatory overlay selection memo
