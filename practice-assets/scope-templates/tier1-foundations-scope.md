# CERG Engagement Scope — Tier 1: Foundations
## Practice Asset — Not a CERG Corpus Document

---

## In Scope
- Organization profile build (`cerg-org-profile.yml`) with token validation
- Full CERG corpus render and adaptation to client names, sector, scale
- Minimum Viable CERG (MVC) adoption: 8 spine documents adapted and signed
- Role consolidation map (1–15 FTEs mapped to canonical role roster)
- Risk register bootstrap: 10+ representative entries with scoring rationale
- First risk register review cycle facilitation
- Maturity baseline assessment per `CERG-GOV-MAT-001`
- Policy sign-off workflow: CISO signs, Executive Sponsor endorses
- Handover package with improvement register

## Out of Scope
- Standards adoption beyond MVC (Access, Logging, Config, etc.)
- Regulatory operational package implementation
- Metrics dashboard design or deployment
- Tooling configuration, procurement, or vendor selection
- Penetration testing or adversarial validation
- Architecture review procedure implementation
- Third-party risk management procedure implementation
- Ongoing advisory beyond handover date

## Deliverables
1. Rendered CERG corpus in client repository
2. Signed Cybersecurity Policy (CERG-POL-001 adapted)
3. Updated Document Catalog with adopted/deferred status
4. Role consolidation map (named person → canonical roles)
5. Risk register with 10+ entries and first review minutes
6. Maturity baseline assessment
7. Improvement register
8. Handover memo

## Duration
30 days from signed engagement. Accelerated to 21 days where client has existing policy framework.

## Price Anchor
Fixed-price engagement. Optional advisory day rate for support beyond handover.
